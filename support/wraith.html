Getting Started

How do I connect?

Open WraithVPN and tap Connect. The app automatically selects the nearest node and establishes a secure WireGuard tunnel in seconds. You will see a connected indicator and your assigned tunnel IP.

Which nodes are available?

WraithVPN Pro includes four global nodes: EU-West (Frankfurt, Germany), EU-North (Helsinki, Finland), APAC (Singapore), and US Central. The free tier connects to one default node. Tap the Nodes screen to view and select manually.

What protocol does WraithVPN use?

WireGuard — a modern, audited VPN protocol using Curve25519 key exchange, ChaCha20 encryption, and Poly1305 authentication. Faster and more efficient than OpenVPN or IKEv2 with minimal battery impact.

Account and Subscription

How do I manage my subscription?

iOS: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > WraithVPN. Android: Play Store > Profile > Subscriptions > WraithVPN. Changes take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

Can I use WraithVPN on multiple devices?

Yes — up to 5 devices per subscription. Log in with the same account on each device.

How do I cancel?

Cancel via Apple Subscriptions or Google Play Subscriptions at least 24 hours before renewal. The subscription remains active through the end of the paid period.

How do I get a refund?

Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com. Android: Google Play support. Web/Stripe: legal@katafract.com.

Privacy and Logs

Do you log my activity?

No. WraithVPN has a strict no-log policy. We do not log traffic, DNS queries, browsing history, real IP address, connection timestamps, or session duration. See our full Privacy Policy.

Can WraithVPN see what I browse?

No. Traffic is encrypted end-to-end via WireGuard. Katafract servers cannot decrypt, inspect, or log your traffic.

What is Haven DNS?

Haven DNS is built-in ad and tracker blocking powered by AdGuard Home, running on every WraithVPN node. It blocks ads, trackers, and malware at the DNS layer before they reach your device. Active automatically whenever you are connected. No individual DNS queries are logged.

Troubleshooting

Connection will not establish.

1. Confirm you have an active internet connection. 2. Try a different node from the Nodes screen. 3. Toggle airplane mode off and on. 4. Force-close and reopen the app. 5. If the issue persists, reinstall from the App Store or Play Store and contact support@katafract.com.

Haven DNS is not blocking ads.

Ensure you are connected to WraithVPN — Haven DNS only works while tunneled. Try switching to a different node. If ads persist, contact support.

Subscription is not restoring on a new device.

Tap Settings in the app and select Restore Purchases. Ensure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

The app is draining my battery.

VPN encryption has some battery overhead. To reduce drain: use Wi-Fi over cellular when possible, disable always-on VPN if not needed, and ensure you are running the latest app version.
